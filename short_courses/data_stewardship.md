# Data Stewardship 

This section contains our short, focused course on Data Stewardship, designed to help you develop the skills required to manage, organise, and share your data.

## Included modules
- **FAIR (meta)data** - Become familiar with the FAIR Principles and learn how you can make your data FAIR. [Clickable Link to Self Study Notes](<FAIR-(meta)data.ipynb>)
- **Data Organisation: Best Practice** - Learn best practice for organising your data. [Clickable Link to Self Study Notes](Data-organisation-and-good-practice.ipynb)
- **Data Publishing** - Understand what data publishing is and how to publish your data. [Clickable Link to Self Study Notes](Data-publishing.ipynb)

## Who is this for?
Learners who want to quickly enhance their data management and stewardship skills, or refresh their knowledge on these topics.

## How to use these notes
Work through the materials in sequence or jump directly to the topic you need. Each module is self-contained and can be completed independently.
